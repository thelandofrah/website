Alan and Joe are a couple of cowboys who started dating in theatre school. 
They find their relationship in tension on the night of their first big play, which they are both co-starring in.
<br><br>
This play: 'Yerdan Gnoccovich & Some Other Broken-Hearted Cowboys',
is a musical that follows an evil bounty hunter on a ruthless chase & two karaoke loving cowboys.
<br><br>
These (karaoke) cowboys sing about Yerdan's broken heart every week but never admit to the breaking of their own hearts when they neglect to acknowledge each other after each song.
<br><br>
Does the script of the play run a little too close to home for Alan & Joe?
<br><br>
Mysterious vagabond director Emeris watches from backstage as the musical unfolds.
